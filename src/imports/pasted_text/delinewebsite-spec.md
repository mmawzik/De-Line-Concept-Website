The prototype currently mixes "De Line Concept," "De line," and "Deline." Lock one

## 2. Color system

The existing palette (navy + cream + muted gold) is the right luxury-architectural
register. Codify it and enforce accent discipline.

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Navy | `#182433` | Anchor sections (pillars block, geography, footer); primary headings on cream |
| Cream | `#F4F0E9` | Dominant page canvas / background |
| Gold (accent) | `#B8956A` | CTA buttons, the 01/02/03 numerals, thin dividers, small labels **only** |
| Warm ink | `#2A2A28` | Body text on cream (not pure black ‚Äî pure black reads harsh on cream) |
| Card surface | `#FBF9F5` | Portfolio + team cards, a hair lighter than canvas for tonal separation |

**Rules**
- Gold stays under ~5% of any screen. Large gold fills kill the luxury read.
- Add one thin gold rule between major sections to break up the long light middle stretch.
- Give cards the lighter `#FBF9F5` surface so the team/portfolio band isn't one flat field.
